watchtower is actionable competitive intelligence

Watchtower is a platform for monitoring changes to your competitor's website and product. Using watchtower is simple and with a few clicks, you can start setting up trackers to monitor when sites change their design, start doing A/B tests, or customize their content to specific users. You do this in a point and click interface so anyone can use this tool to keep track of and respond to the competition regardless of technical ability.

Watchtower was built at the Launch 2013 by a team of folks from Room 77.
