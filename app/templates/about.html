Actionable Competitive Intelligence with Watchtower

Watchtower is a platform for monitoring changes to your competition's website and products. With a few clicks, you can set up trackers to monitor changes to site look and feel, content, and pricing.

Using cloud-based virtual displays, Watchtower detects A/B tests, multivariate tests, and other forms of customer segmentation. We also find price changes and other content changes.

Watchtower was originally built at the 2013 LAUNCH hackathon.
